PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
Benefit: Rs 6000 per year given in 3 instalments of Rs 2000 each directly to bank account.
Eligibility: All landholding farmer families with cultivable land. Small and marginal farmers.
How to apply: Visit pmkisan.gov.in or nearest Common Service Centre (CSC).
Documents needed: Aadhaar card, bank account, land records (Khatoni).

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) — Crop Insurance
Benefit: Insurance cover for crop losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.
Premium: Only 2% for Kharif crops, 1.5% for Rabi crops, 5% for commercial crops.
How to apply: Through your nearest bank branch or at pmfby.gov.in before crop season starts.
Claim: Report crop damage to local agriculture officer or insurer within 72 hours.

Soil Health Card Scheme
Benefit: Free soil testing. Card shows which nutrients your soil needs and how much fertilizer to use.
How to get: Visit your nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) or agriculture department office.
Frequency: New card issued every 2 years.
