Tomato
Kharif season: Sow seeds in June-July. Transplant seedlings in July-August. Harvest October-November.
Rabi season: Sow seeds in October-November. Harvest February-March.
Ideal temperature: 20-27°C. Avoid planting when temperature exceeds 35°C.
Spacing: 60cm between rows, 45cm between plants.

Rice (Paddy)
Kharif (Samba): Transplant June-August. Harvest November-January.
Rabi (Kuruvai): Transplant February-April. Harvest May-July.
Water requirement: 1200-1400mm per crop. Maintain 5cm standing water in vegetative stage.

Onion
Kharif: Sow June-July. Harvest October-November. Best for South India.
Rabi: Sow November-December. Harvest March-April.
Note: Avoid waterlogged soils. Reduce irrigation 2 weeks before harvest for better storage.

Wheat
Sow: November to first week of December (Rabi crop).
Harvest: March-April.
Ideal temperature for germination: 20-25°C. Needs cold weather for flowering.
