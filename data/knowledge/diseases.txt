Early Blight (Alternaria solani)
Symptoms: Dark brown spots with concentric rings on older leaves. Yellowing around spots.
Causes: High humidity above 80%, warm temperatures 24-29°C.
Pesticide: Chlorothalonil 2g per litre of water OR Mancozeb 2.5g per litre.
Application: Spray every 7 to 10 days. Spray in the morning. Avoid wetting leaves at night.
Prevention: Rotate crops every season. Remove and burn infected leaves immediately.
Organic option: Neem oil 5ml per litre spray every 5 days.

Late Blight (Phytophthora infestans)
Symptoms: Water-soaked patches on leaves turning dark brown. White mould on underside.
Causes: Cool moist weather, temperatures 15-20°C with rain.
Pesticide: Metalaxyl plus Mancozeb 3g per litre OR Cymoxanil 0.8g per litre.
Application: Begin spraying at first sign. Repeat every 5 days during wet weather.
Prevention: Use resistant varieties. Avoid overhead irrigation. Space plants for air flow.

===== RICE DISEASES =====

Rice Blast (Magnaporthe oryzae)
Symptoms: Diamond-shaped lesions with grey centres and brown borders on leaves.
Pesticide: Tricyclazole 0.6g per litre OR Isoprothiolane 1.5ml per litre.
Application: Spray at tillering stage and again at booting stage.
Prevention: Do not over-apply nitrogen fertilizer. Use blast-resistant varieties.

===== WHEAT DISEASES =====

Wheat Rust (Puccinia species)
Symptoms: Orange-red or yellow powdery pustules on leaves and stems.
Pesticide: Propiconazole 1ml per litre OR Tebuconazole 1ml per litre.
Application: Spray at first appearance. Repeat after 15 days if needed.
Prevention: Sow early. Use certified rust-resistant seed varieties.
